About

Clinical philosophy & background

A brief overview of who I am, how I practice, and what matters to me when a patient enters the Emergency Department.

I trained and worked in high-acuity Emergency Departments across India and the UK. My work ranges from managing time-critical conditions like myocardial infarction, stroke, sepsis and trauma, to building robust ED systems and pathways.

My approach combines calm bedside care with a strong focus on process: clear triage, rapid diagnosis, protocol-driven treatment, and continuous communication with patients, relatives, and multidisciplinary teams.

Alongside clinical work, I am deeply involved in quality improvement, ED protocol design, staff training, and integrating technology and preventive health concepts into everyday care.
